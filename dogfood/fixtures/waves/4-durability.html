Does it survive being interrupted?

Wave 4 certifies coordination and durability: the site regenerates byte-identically from git, durable jobs survive a SIGKILL and resume without rework, migration refuses silent overwrites, and abort actually aborts. Every drill broke something real first — f022 through f026 — and every finding was fixed and locked in the same session. That is the point of drills.

What Wave 4 is (plain language)

Waves 1–3 proved single commands. Wave 4 proves work that spans time and can be interrupted:

  1. Construction idempotence — the full committed construct.yaml regenerates this entire site; two consecutive regens leave every public route byte-identical.
  2. Durable jobs — a 21-task job SIGKILLed at 7/21 must resume to 21/21 with every task attempts: 1: no rework, no double-processing.
  3. Migration — imports normalize to servable routes, batch collisions are refused up front with the url→path table, and validation checks against the known source.
  4. Abort means abort — pipeline abort mid-flight on the real 74-step regen must stop the run at the batch boundary, not quietly finish it.

What the drills broke (and locked)

  • f022 — "durable" jobs could not survive the one thing they exist for: a stale lock after SIGKILL crashed resume. Fixed with a pid-liveness reclaim probe.
  • f023 — explicit --path skipped .html normalization: unservable content reported "imported".
  • f024 — colliding derived batch paths silently overwrote, last writer wins, all lines "done". Now refused up front.
  • f025 — per-step saves rewrote run state from memory and clobbered the abort marker: aborted runs completed through their own abort.
  • f026 — shipping the f022–f025 pages surfaced deploy printing two bare arrays on a flawless run: the agent could not parse its own success.

Honest note on idempotence: baseline hashes are CLI-version-scoped. The hardening-pass CLI changes altered put serialization for two routes against the opening-rep baselines; idempotence is proven per-version and holds at the version Wave 4 cut with (see the evidence README).

Residue (tracked, not blockers): pipeline run --job (durable pipeline runs) unbuilt; the f017 envelope-migration march continues.
