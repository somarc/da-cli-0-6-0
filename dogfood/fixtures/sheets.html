Why these live outside the git repo's evidence packs

Evidence packs under dogfood/evidence/wave-N/ capture what a command said at a moment in time; these endpoints are what the platform says now. The gap between the two is exactly where Wave 5 findings live: a claimed migration that was never on the platform (f029), an index that froze while pages kept publishing (C3). When this page and the evidence disagree, that disagreement is a drill waiting to run.
