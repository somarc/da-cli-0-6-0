f006 · route · fixed

Deleted source still classified as contentbus

Symptom

Wave 3 built a deliberate orphan: publish a page, then delete its DA source, leaving a live artifact with no owner. Classify missed it — verdict contentbus from a stale content.da.live sourceLocation, even with daSource: false.

Why it matters

Orphan detection is the backbone of route clean and hybrid-site safety. False contentbus ownership hides debris.

Resolution

The DA source probe is ground truth. daSource: false never yields contentbus, even when preview/live still advertise a DA location. Those cases classify as orphan with staleDaLocation: true.

Lock

Unit tests and live /tests/orphan-demo.
